About Us

Club Info

Welcome to SKN IEEE Student Branch. Founded in 2021, we are a community of tech enthusiasts, innovators, and learners. Our mission is to inspire and empower students to make an impact through technology. We currently have 50+ dedicated members actively participating in our club's activities.

Our club hosts a wide range of events, workshops, and projects to enhance technical/non-technical knowledge and foster innovation among our members.
